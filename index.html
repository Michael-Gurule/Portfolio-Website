About Me

9+ Years of Experience

Passionate about turning data into intelligence

I'm a data scientist and ML engineer with 9 years of experience turning sophisticated algorithms into systems that actually work in production. My career started in Data Strategy consulting at firms like JS Held and. Sedgwick, where I learned to translate between executive priorities and technical execution. That foundation taught me something crucial: the best model in the world means nothing if it can't ship.

These days, I split my time between building production ML systems and completing my MS in Data Science at University of Colorado. My projects span financial optimization, aerospace defense applications, and multi-sensor intelligence platforms. Each one focuses on the same principle: technical depth matched with practical deployment.

Currently based in the Denver area and open to remote opportunities. I'm particularly interested in roles where I can apply deep technical skills to meaningful problems, whether that's in defense, finance, or emerging tech applications.
